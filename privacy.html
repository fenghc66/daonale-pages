1.3 Special Note on Location Data

Location data is the core functionality of this App. We want you to clearly understand:

  • Foreground Location: When the App is open, we collect your location approximately every 5 seconds.
  • Background Location: When the App runs in the background, we collect your location approximately every 60 seconds to keep your circle members informed.
  • Privacy Level Controls: You can switch your location sharing at any time:
    • Precise (L1): Share exact GPS coordinates
    • Fuzzy (L2): Share only an approximate area (1-2 km radius)
    • Frozen (L3): Show only last known position and timestamp; no further updates
  • Leaving a Circle: When you leave a circle, location sharing with that circle's members stops immediately.
  • Data Retention: Only the most recent location is stored in real-time. Location history is retained for 7 days (free users) or up to 90 days (premium users), then automatically deleted.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly under the principles of lawfulness, fairness, and data minimization:

  1. Core Services: Display member locations on the map, geofence entry/exit alerts, SOS emergency notifications.
  2. Account Management: Registration, login, identity verification, account security.
  3. Product Improvement: Analyze usage data to improve location accuracy, reduce power consumption, and enhance user experience.
  4. Safety & Security: Prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats.
  5. Legal Compliance: Respond to lawful requests from law enforcement or regulators.

Our Commitments:

  • We will NEVER sell your location data to any third party
  • We will NEVER use location data for advertising or behavioral profiling
  • We will NEVER activate location tracking without your knowledge

5. Your Rights

5.1 Access and Correction

You can view and update your personal information in the App's Settings page.

5.2 Deletion

  • Delete location history within the App (one-click clear)
  • Delete your account to remove all associated data

5.3 Withdraw Consent

  • Revoke location permissions in your device settings at any time (core features will be unavailable)
  • Leave any circle to stop sharing your location with its members
  • Switch your privacy level to "Frozen" to pause location updates

5.4 Account Deletion

You can request account deletion in "Settings > Account > Delete Account". Upon deletion:

  • All personal information will be deleted within 30 days
  • You will be removed from all circles
  • Purchased premium services cannot be recovered
