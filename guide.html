NearU User Guide

Getting Started

Download & Install

  • Download from Google Play or direct APK from our download page
  • Open the app and grant location permissions when prompted

Create Your Account

  • Sign up with email (international) or phone number (China)
  • Set your display name and avatar

Core Features

Create a Circle

  • Tap "+" to create a new circle
  • Choose type: Family / Couples / Travel Group / Team
  • Share the invite code, QR code, or link with members

Join a Circle

  • Scan QR code, enter invite code, or tap invite link
  • Accept the invitation to start sharing location

View Members on Map

  • The home screen shows all circle members on the map
  • Tap a member to see their details: last update time, battery level, address
  • Switch between circles using the circle selector

Privacy Controls

Three Privacy Levels

  • Precise (L1): Share your exact GPS location
  • Fuzzy (L2): Share approximate area (1-2km radius)
  • Frozen (L3): Show last known location only, no updates

How to Change Privacy Level

  • Tap your avatar on the map → Privacy Settings
  • Select the desired level per circle
  • Changes take effect immediately

Safety Features

SOS Emergency

  • Long-press the SOS button for 2 seconds
  • Your precise location is immediately shared with all circle members
  • A push notification is sent to everyone in your circles

Geofence Alerts

  • Go to Settings → Geofences → Add New
  • Select a location on the map and set the radius
  • Choose alert type: arrival, departure, or both
  • Notifications are sent when members enter or leave the area

Account Management

Edit Profile

  • Settings → Profile → Edit name, avatar

Delete Location History

  • Settings → Privacy → Clear History

Delete Account

  • Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • All data is removed within 30 days

FAQ

Does NearU drain my battery?

No. We use a smart location strategy: high-frequency updates when the app is active, low-frequency in the background.

Can I stop sharing my location?

Yes. Switch to Frozen mode, leave the circle, or revoke location permissions in your device settings.

Is my location data sold to third parties?

Never. We do not sell, share, or use your location data for advertising.

What happens when I leave a circle?

Your location is immediately hidden from all members in that circle.

Which countries are supported?

NearU works in 180+ countries. We use AMap for China and OpenStreetMap/Google Maps internationally.
